These are the datasets that the different chroniclers (AI agents) will read to create the timeline of events according to their personalities.
More precisely, there are stock market tweets (https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/dawoodaijaz/stock-market-tweets-labelled-with-gcp-nlp), disaster tweets were collected on Jan 14th, 2020 (https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/vstepanenko/disaster-tweets), covid 19 tweets (https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/arunavakrchakraborty/covid19-twitter-dataset), and lastly, to add a little bit of variance into the thinking of the agents, tweets of a well-known Asian game developer, Hideo Kojima (https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/luciesteel/kojima-tweets-en).
